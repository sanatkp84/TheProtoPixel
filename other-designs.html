Understanding Aspect Ratios

Different platforms and design types require specific aspect ratios. Our system preserves your original image and video ratios to ensure optimal display across all devices and platforms.

Design Type Common Aspect Ratios Typical Use
YouTube Thumbnails 16:9 YouTube video thumbnails
Instagram Posts 1:1 (Square) Standard Instagram feed posts
Instagram Stories/Reels 9:16 Vertical content for mobile viewing
Facebook Cover 16:9 or 2.7:1 Facebook page header images
Twitter Header 3:1 Twitter profile header images
LinkedIn Banner 4:1 LinkedIn profile and company page headers
